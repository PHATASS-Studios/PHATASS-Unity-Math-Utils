		==== IMPORTANT NOTES ====

Never include *.meta files in this repository.
Never include MonoBehaviours, ScriptableObjects or UnityEngine.Objects in this repository.
Lack of *.meta files would break their behaviour and containing projects.

Only utily classes, interfaces, and extension methods must be included.
